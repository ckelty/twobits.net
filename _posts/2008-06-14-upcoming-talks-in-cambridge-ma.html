I'm giving two presentations about the book in Cambridge before I leave for the year.

The first will be at the Berkman Center for Internet and Society's lunch series. I've been hanging out at the Berkman Center off and on this year, and I'm eager to hear from the smart people who hang out there. June 17, noon-thirty.

The other will be as part of the MIT Press Bookstore's Authors@MIT series. I'm especially proud of this one because once upon a time I worked at the MIT Press bookstore, and often helped organize exactly this kind of event. It's like coming home... I often say that I would never have gotten to this point without that job; i've never felt so tuned in to the state of scholarship as when I spent 20hrs a week thinking about new books... I doubt I will ever be as hip as I was working there... June 24th 5:30pm
